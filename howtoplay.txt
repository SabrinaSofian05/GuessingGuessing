HOW TO PLAY
------------
1. Enter your name to make sure you have log in to the game.
2. Read the instruction.
3. Guess word according to the total of spaces required.

~Attention
- Do not type only 1 character. Please type the whole word that you can guess.
- There is only 6 attempt. After that, the answer will automatically popped out.

Thank you for reading this. Please enjoy the game. Goodluck!
